A Symphony of Flavors: Crafting the Perfect Christmas Cocktails


'Tis the season to be jolly, and what better way to spread holiday cheer than with a selection of Christmas cocktails that will dazzle your taste buds? From warm and comforting to cool and refreshing, these festive concoctions are bound to elevate your celebrations. As the snow falls and the lights twinkle, indulge in the art of mixology with a variety of Christmas-inspired libations. Whether you prefer the classic warmth of a spiced concoction or the zesty freshness of a fruity blend, there's a Christmas cocktail for every palate. Capture the spirit of the season with creative combinations of spirits, mixers, and garnishes. Experiment with herbs, spices, and seasonal fruits to create your signature drink that will leave a lasting impression on your guests...

Alcoholic Cocktails

Christmas Spritz

TMix Aperol, sparkling wine and bitters with ginger beer and garnish with orange, star anise and pomegranate seeds to make this festive spritz.

Gingerbread Espresso Martini

Shake up your winter drinks party in more ways than one with this gingerbread and espresso martini cocktail. Velvety smooth coffee and festive spice syrup make this the perfect after-dinner warmer. Check out our best ever Christmas dessert recipes to indulge in this Christmas...

Hot buttered rum

Treat yourself this Christmas to a hot buttered rum, mixing golden rum with soft light brown sugar, unsalted butter and festive spices.

Sloe sazerac

A seriously punchy whisky cocktail perfect for slow sipping during the winter months. A splash of sloe gin adds a festive twit to this classic.

Non-alcoholic Cocktails

Crock-Pot Hot Chocolate

Making your hot chocolate in a slow cooker is an easy way to serve a crowd. Not only will it yield enough mugs for the whole family, but it can also keep the drinks warm while you decorate the tree!

Shirley Temple

This classic drink has been a popular choice at the kids table for generations. Even Ree Drummond remembers enjoying a glass of the sweet, bubbly cherry drink during her childhood.

Christmas Morning Punch

This holiday punch is made with a combination of fruit juices, like orange, pineapple, and pomegranate, plus sparkling apple juice. Plus, ice cubes made with pomegranate seeds and rosemary sprigs add some extra holiday magic.

Apple Nutmeg Mocktail

TInspired by a whisky sour, this non-alcoholic version is fruity, spicy, and foamy on top! Plus, it's vegan for those who don't like the idea of using egg whites for the foam.
